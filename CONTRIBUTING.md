# Contributing

Legal OS welcomes focused improvements to generic workflows, Skills, validation scripts, documentation and synthetic-safe tests.

## Keep contributions public-safe

Do not submit client files, matter facts, real names, contact information, case numbers, credentials, private paths, privileged material, confidential templates or internal strategy. Use synthetic or irreversibly anonymized examples only.

## Pull requests

Please explain the problem, the proposed change, the affected module, the verification performed and any known limitations. Keep unrelated refactors separate. If a change affects a quality gate, interface, version boundary or public/private boundary, call that out explicitly.

## Legal and factual content

Do not present an unverified current-law proposition as settled. Identify jurisdiction and sources where a contribution depends on legal authority, and keep drafting, review, authorization, filing, sending and signing as separate states.
