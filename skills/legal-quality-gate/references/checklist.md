# Legal Quality Gate Checklist

## 1. Fact Gate

- Party names and legal identity are consistent.
- The represented or reviewing party and the counterparty are correctly identified.
- Amounts, dates, contract numbers, project names, case numbers, court/arbitration bodies, and procedural stages match current materials.
- Payment, delivery, acceptance, warranty, invoice, breach, termination, settlement, withdrawal, and enforcement facts are evidenced.
- Old memory is treated only as a clue unless current material confirms it.

## 2. Evidence Gate

- Every claim in a formal document maps to evidence or is removed.
- Evidence catalog proof items correspond to claims, defenses, or disputed issues.
- Communications, demand letters, meeting notes, and negotiation records are not overstated as proof of completion, acceptance, or debt confirmation unless they actually prove that point.
- Page numbers are left blank or marked pending if not confirmed.

## 3. Legal Authority Gate

- Chinese legal authorities are current and effective.
- Key provisions that materially affect the conclusion are quoted or accurately summarized according to document length.
- Cases, policy guidance, local rules, public registration, enforcement information, and qualification information are verified before use.
- Third-party legal skills or old notes are not treated as sources of law.

## 4. Expression Gate

- Formal text contains no internal strategy, risk memo, backup argument, AI/process note, temporary path, OCR note, or model judgment.
- No unnecessary admission of fault, concession, waiver, settlement signal, or acceptance of changed commercial terms.
- Tone is restrained, factual, and suitable for the recipient.
- Government/court materials are not written as emotional accusations.

## 5. Format Gate

- Word/PDF/Excel/table deliverables are complete, readable, and suitable for direct submission or leadership circulation.
- Titles, numbering, fonts, spacing, tables, signatures, dates, and page numbers are coherent.
- Evidence catalogs use the correct simple or complex table according to task needs.
- Final external versions do not include comments, tracked-change debris, internal notes, or unverified placeholders.
