# Risk-calibrated decision-question ladder

Use this ladder to ask only questions that require the user to choose between material paths. Fact requests, file requests, and authority-verification work are tracked separately and do not consume the decision budget.

| Risk | Normal decision-question budget | Escalation rule |
| --- | --- | --- |
| R0 | 0 | Ask only if the task cannot be routed without a choice. |
| R1 | 0-1 | Ask one focused question when the intended work product or audience changes the route. |
| R2 | 1-2 | Ask about the material legal or commercial choice, not every missing fact. |
| R3 | 3-8 in stages | Stop and stage the interview when major branches remain unresolved. |

After two R2 decision questions, reclassify to R3 if a material branch remains unresolved. An external-action request creates an authorization blocker but does not by itself raise R2 to R3. Record the interview mode and questions in the routing header.
