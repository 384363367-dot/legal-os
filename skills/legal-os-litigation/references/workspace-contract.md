# Workspace contract

The litigation stack is layered:

`Litigation Analysis → Evidence Workspace → Legal Research → Paired Pleading + Evidence Catalogue Workspace`

Each layer consumes identified inputs and emits traceable artifacts. A later layer may not silently supply a missing earlier-layer fact. Complaints, applications and answers are paired with an independent evidence catalogue in one drafting run; the pleading does not duplicate that catalogue as an evidence-source chapter. The public repository may describe schemas and controls, but private matter files remain outside the public repository.
