# Labour arbitration template authority note

## Cost rule

- Authority: Article 53, *PRC Labour Dispute Mediation and Arbitration Law*.
- Proposition: labour-dispute arbitration is free of charge; the arbitration commission's funding is guaranteed by public finance.
- Official source: National People's Congress, <https://www.npc.gov.cn/npc/c2/c183/c198/201905/t20190522_28233.html>
- Accessed: 2026-07-20.
- Template consequence: a labour/personnel arbitration application or answer must not include a request that the opposing party bear labour-arbitration costs.

Recheck current validity before relying on this authority in a case-specific final submission. The generic template rule does not replace matter-level legal research.
