# Workspace contract

The formal-correspondence layer consumes current, identified material from the relevant Contract, Litigation, Evidence, and Legal Research workspaces. It emits an internal analysis package, a draft, an internal review version, an external clean version, and a separately authorized sending/receipt record.

Template availability never authorizes sending, service, filing, signing, or other external action. A missing or contradictory material input is a stop or review state, not a prompt to infer.
