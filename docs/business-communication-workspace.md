# Legal OS Business Communication Workspace

Generic, source-locked workflow for Chinese business communication. It covers internal messages, business WeChat, email, phone or meeting talking points, and ordinary working communication that is not a formal legal notice.

## Runtime contract

1. Read the request and every in-scope file.
2. Identify the audience, channel, objective, role, verified facts, output form and sending permission.
3. Select one T-07 path: leadership update, project coordination, internal cross-team message, ordinary counterparty communication, or phone/meeting talking points.
4. Load only necessary contract, litigation, evidence, current-law research, data-verification or document-QC helpers.
5. Draft purpose first, then verified facts, requested action, owner/deadline and reply method.
6. Scan for unauthorized admissions, concessions, new prices, fixed deadlines, settlement, waiver, release, termination or external commitments.
7. Route formal notices to the formal-correspondence workflow, current-law assertions to legal research, amount/date conflicts to data verification, and formal leadership reports or presentations to reporting.

## Hard stops

Pause when core facts conflict, evidence is missing, external authority is unclear, a rights-impacting commitment may be made, or sending/confidentiality boundaries are not clear. Return a labelled draft or focused questions; do not guess.

## Public boundary

This public workflow contains no client names, matter facts, private paths, credentials, contract text or unverified legal conclusions.
