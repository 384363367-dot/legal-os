# Formal Correspondence Workspace

This module provides a source-locked workflow for generic Chinese formal correspondence. It is a template and quality-gate layer, not a sending or filing service.

## Supported forms

- Lawyer letter
- Payment or performance notice
- Reply letter
- Situation statement

## Required inputs

Use user-confirmed sender and recipient details, the relevant contract or matter materials, evidence supporting each material assertion, and current verified legal authority for any legal proposition. Record the deadline, calculation basis, delivery channel, and attachments separately.

## Quality gate

Before an external clean version is approved, verify:

- purpose, audience, sender and recipient;
- facts, amounts, dates, obligations and evidence mapping;
- deadline, delivery method, attachments and requested response;
- current legal authority where law is cited;
- removal of internal strategy and process notes; and
- separate authorization for drafting, review, sending, service, filing and signing.

Hold the document when a material fact or deadline is missing or contradictory, or when the text could create an admission, waiver, settlement, termination effect, or other rights-changing result.

## Release boundary

The public module contains generic workflow guidance only. Real matter files, client information, private evidence, internal strategy and sending records remain private.
