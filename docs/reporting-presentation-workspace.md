# Legal OS Reporting and Presentation Workspace

This is a generic, source-locked workflow for turning matter updates into status reports and editable presentations. It does not create facts, replace legal or financial review, or send material externally.

## Core method

- Lock the matter, reporting period, audience, cadence, workstreams, owners, and baseline sources.
- Extract progress, dependencies, decisions, risks, amounts, and dates; identify silent workstreams and vague updates.
- Apply Green/Amber/Red with reasons. Amber needs a return-to-green action; Red needs escalation and remediation.
- Give every next action an owner and target date.
- Keep facts, judgments, risks, and recommendations visibly separate.

## Report and presentation outputs

Internal reports lead with exceptions and decisions. Client reports provide the full picture and frame issues as impact plus mitigation. Leadership presentations lead with the conclusion, use one message per page, preserve source notes, and deliver an editable PPTX plus PNG preview when requested.

## Hard stops

Pause when scope, period, audience, baseline, source, numbers, dates, versions, or owners conflict; when a RAG conclusion lacks support; when internal strategy or unapproved commitments would enter an external version; or when sending, submitting, publishing, or overwriting is requested without authorization.

Amounts and date conflicts route to data verification. Substantive legal or business changes route to the responsible primary workflow. File conversion, privacy cleaning, page checks, and archiving route to the file-delivery workflow.
